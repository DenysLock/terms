Contact & Help

Flexory: Workout Tracker

Need help or have questions? We're here to assist you! Whether you need technical support, want to report a bug, or have suggestions for new features, don't hesitate to reach out to our team.

Get in Touch

For all inquiries, support requests, and feedback:

contact@flexoryapp.org

We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days.

Technical Support

Having trouble with the app? We're here to help! When contacting us for technical support, please include:

  • Your device type and operating system version
  • App version you're using
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots if applicable

This information helps us resolve your issue faster and more efficiently.

Report Bugs

Found a bug or something not working as expected? We appreciate your help in making Flexory better! Please report any issues you encounter, including:

  • App crashes or freezing
  • Data not saving correctly
  • Features not working properly
  • Display or interface issues
  • Performance problems

Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new feature or improvement? We love hearing from our users! Share your suggestions for:

  • New workout tracking features
  • UI/UX improvements
  • Integration with other fitness apps
  • Social features and sharing options
  • Advanced analytics and reporting

Your feedback helps shape the future of Flexory!

General Feedback

We value your opinion! Whether you want to share what you love about Flexory or suggest areas for improvement, we're always eager to hear from our community.

Your feedback helps us understand how we can make Flexory the best workout tracking app possible.

Business Inquiries

For partnership opportunities, press inquiries, or other business-related matters, please reach out to us at our main contact email. We'll make sure your message gets to the right team member.

Privacy & Data

Questions about how we handle your data? Check out our Privacy Policy or contact us directly. We're committed to transparency about our data practices and protecting your privacy.

For data deletion requests or other privacy-related inquiries, please contact us with "Privacy Request" in the subject line.
