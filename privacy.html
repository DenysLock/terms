Privacy Policy

Flexory: Workout Tracker
Last Updated: June 10, 2025

We take your privacy seriously! This privacy policy explains how Flexory collects, uses, and shares personal information of users of the Flexory app. By using the Flexory app, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this privacy policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about how we collect and process your Personal Data, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at contact@flexoryapp.org.

Collection of Personal Information

When you register for an account with Flexory, we collect your email address, username, and password. We also collect any personal information that you choose to include in your profile, such as your name, profile image, and other optional information.

When you use the app, we also collect information about your workout activities, including:

  • Workout data and exercise information
  • Progress tracking data
  • Workout notes and preferences
  • Usage patterns and app interactions

Use of Personal Information

We use the personal information we collect to:

  • Provide you with access to the Flexory app and its features
  • Track your workout progress and fitness goals
  • Communicate with you about your use of the app
  • Personalize your experience on the app
  • Provide you with information about new features or updates
  • Improve the app and our services
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of our services

Sharing of Personal Information

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who help us operate the app and provide you with services, such as hosting and cloud storage providers. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only permitted to use your data as necessary to provide their services to us.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information for analytics and improvement purposes.

Storage and Security of Personal Information

Your personal information is stored securely on our servers and on the servers of our trusted third-party service providers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data during transmission over the internet. By using our platform, you acknowledge and accept this inherent risk.

Access and Control of Personal Information

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access and update your personal information in the app's settings
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and personal information from the app at any time by contacting our support team
  • Correction: You can correct any inaccurate personal information
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@flexoryapp.org.

Collection and Use of Workout Data

When you log workouts in the Flexory app, we collect:

  • Exercise types and workout routines
  • Duration and intensity of workouts
  • Progress measurements and achievements
  • Personal notes and workout preferences
  • Timestamps of workout sessions

We use this data to provide you with personalized workout recommendations, track your progress over time, and help you achieve your fitness goals.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Information Regarding Children

Our platform is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 13, please report your concerns to us immediately at contact@flexoryapp.org.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. Your continued use of the Flexory app after such modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
